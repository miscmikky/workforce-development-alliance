# Skills Gap Analysis & Training Coordination Platform\n\n## Overview\n\nThis pull request introduces a comprehensive blockchain-based platform for workforce development, specifically designed to address skills gap analysis and training coordination challenges in the modern job market.\n\n## Smart Contracts Implemented\n\n### 1. Skills Analysis Contract (`skills-analysis.clar`)\n\n**Purpose**: Manages skill demand registration, supply tracking, and gap analysis functionality.\n\n**Key Features**:\n- **Skills Registry**: Complete catalog of skills with categories and descriptions\n- **Demand Tracking**: Employers register skill requirements with urgency levels and salary ranges\n- **Supply Management**: Workers register their skills with proficiency levels and certifications\n- **Gap Analysis**: Automated identification and severity assessment of skill shortages\n- **Verification System**: Admin verification for employers and skill providers\n\n**Core Functions**:\n- `register-skill`: Add new skills to the system registry\n- `register-employer`: Employer onboarding with company verification\n- `register-provider`: Worker/skill provider registration\n- `register-skill-demand`: Employers specify skill requirements and urgency\n- `register-skill-supply`: Workers declare available skills and experience\n- `create-skill-gap`: System-generated analysis of market shortages\n- `update-skill-gap`: Dynamic updates to gap analysis\n- `verify-employer` / `verify-provider`: Admin verification functions\n\n**Data Structures**:\n- Skills: 50+ character names, 30-character categories, 200-character descriptions\n- Demands: 1-10 demand scale, 1-5 urgency scale, salary ranges, experience requirements\n- Supply: 1-10 proficiency scale, years of experience, availability status\n- Gaps: Severity calculations, affected locations, recommended actions\n\n### 2. Training Coordinator Contract (`training-coordinator.clar`)\n\n**Purpose**: Manages training programs, participant enrollment, and certification processes.\n\n**Key Features**:\n- **Provider Registration**: Training institutions and individual trainers\n- **Program Management**: Comprehensive training program lifecycle\n- **Enrollment System**: Participant registration and progress tracking\n- **Certification**: Blockchain-verified training certificates\n- **Progress Tracking**: Real-time monitoring of training completion\n- **Rating System**: Provider performance evaluation\n\n**Core Functions**:\n- `register-training-provider`: Training institution onboarding\n- `register-participant`: Learner registration with skill levels and goals\n- `create-training-program`: Comprehensive program setup with schedules\n- `enroll-in-program`: Participant enrollment with capacity management\n- `update-progress`: Real-time progress tracking by providers\n- `complete-program`: Program completion with scoring and feedback\n- `issue-certificate`: Blockchain certificate issuance\n- `verify-certificate`: Public certificate verification\n- `drop-from-program`: Participant withdrawal handling\n\n**Data Structures**:\n- Programs: 100-character titles, 300-character descriptions, difficulty levels 1-5\n- Enrollments: Status tracking (enrolled/in-progress/completed/dropped), percentage completion\n- Certificates: SHA-256 hashes for verification, skill levels achieved, expiry dates\n- Participants: Career goals, completion counts, certificate totals\n\n## Technical Implementation\n\n### Architecture Design\n- **No Cross-Contract Dependencies**: Each contract operates independently for maximum security\n- **Comprehensive Data
